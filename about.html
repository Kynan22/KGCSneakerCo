Our Story

KGC Sneaker Co. is the new up and coming shoe brand taking the world by storm!

As soon as we started we have been expanding our store base and perfecting our design. Today people need a reliable shoe that looks the best and does it's job even better and that's what we're here to provide.

We were established in 2010 and have powered through the ranks to become Australia's #1 Shoe Brand. We have 5 locations around Australia and are expecting more in a few years, KGC Sneaker Co. wants to make their shoes as accessible and available as possible so now if you cannot make it into one of our stores you can jump online and make your purchase of the newest trends here!

This is just the beginning.
